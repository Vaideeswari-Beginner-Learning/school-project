Our Facilities

Our school provides a safe and well-equipped environment to support students in both academic learning and physical development.

Library

Our school library provides a wide collection of books, reference materials, and educational magazines that encourage students to develop reading habits and improve their knowledge.

Computer Lab

The computer lab is equipped with modern computers and internet facilities to help students learn basic computer skills and technology.

Science Laboratory

Separate laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology allow students to perform practical experiments and understand scientific concepts effectively.

Smart Classrooms

Digital smart boards and multimedia tools make learning interactive and engaging for students.

Sports Playground

A spacious playground supports sports activities like cricket, football, athletics, volleyball, and badminton.

Transport Facility

Our school transport system provides safe and reliable bus services covering nearby areas.

Safety & Security

The campus is monitored through CCTV cameras and managed with proper safety measures to ensure a secure environment for students.
