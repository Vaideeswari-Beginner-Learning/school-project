Sports & Physical Education

At our Matriculation Higher Secondary School, sports play an important role in developing discipline, teamwork, and leadership among students. We encourage every student to actively participate in physical activities and sports competitions.

Sports Programs

Cricket

Our cricket team regularly participates in inter-school and district-level tournaments. Students receive proper training and practice sessions to improve their skills.

Football

Football encourages teamwork, stamina, and strategy. Our school football team competes in various inter-school matches.

Basketball

Basketball helps students develop coordination and teamwork while improving physical fitness.

Volleyball

Volleyball is one of the most popular sports in our school and students actively participate in tournaments and competitions.

Athletics

Athletics events such as running, long jump, and relay races help students build speed, endurance, and strength.

Badminton

Badminton improves reflexes and focus, and students practice regularly under supervision.
