Academic Programs

Our academic curriculum focuses on building strong foundations in education while encouraging creativity, discipline, and critical thinking.

Primary Section

Classes I – V

The primary section focuses on basic literacy, numeracy, and communication skills through activity-based learning methods.

Middle School

Classes VI – VIII

Middle school education strengthens students’ understanding of subjects such as Mathematics, Science, and Languages while promoting curiosity and creativity.

High School

Classes IX – X

Students are prepared for SSLC board examinations with structured teaching methods, regular assessments, and practical learning.

Higher Secondary

Classes XI – XII

Our higher secondary programs help students prepare for higher education and future career opportunities. Available Groups:

  • 🔬 Biology
  • 💻 Computer Science
  • 📈 Commerce
  • 📐 Mathematics
